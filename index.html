Services across the lifecycle

EPC execution plus engineering, verification and grid integration support that reduces technical risk from design to operations. BESS first. PV where relevant.

Turnkey EPC(F)

Engineering, procurement, construction and verified system integration for grid-scale, C&I and hybrid projects.

  • Single line diagram (SLD) and electrical topology
  • Civil, MV works, protection, PPC/EMS/SCADA integration
  • Commissioning, performance test and handover documentation

Project development support

Early phase work to reach RTB with realistic schedules, risks and interface clarity.

  • Site and grid studies coordination (scope, inputs, assumptions)
  • Layout planning: access, setbacks, boundaries, PoC concept
  • Stakeholder alignment and tender readiness

Grid integration and compliance

Interface management and evidence packages for VDE-AR-N 4110/4120/4130 and EN 50549.

  • Protection and control concept, settings philosophy
  • FAT/SAT support and grid-code evidence package
  • Remote interface coordination: PPC/EMS/SCADA/utility

Technical management

Engineering leadership across batteries, PCS, transformers, EMS and SCADA with clear interface control.

  • Tender evaluation and technical clarifications
  • Contractor coordination and punch-list governance
  • Risk register and mitigation tracking

Quality assurance and verification

Incoming goods checks, installation verification and acceptance readiness to protect warranty and reduce rework.

  • Waren-Eingang checks: components + documentation
  • Installation verification and corrective actions
  • Acceptance readiness review with evidence structure

Commissioning support and handover

Commissioning readiness reviews, test planning, defect closure tracking and structured handover documentation.

  • Cold/hot commissioning readiness and test plans
  • 72h run support and performance verification (as applicable)
  • Handover pack: protocols, settings, as-built, certificates

Technical due diligence support

Independent assessment of design, interfaces and compliance gaps for buyers, investors and lenders.

  • Document review and gap analysis (grid, protection, interfaces)
  • Transparent risk findings with practical actions
  • Bankable reporting structure and evidence traceability

Owner’s engineering support

Owner representation during execution to control quality, schedule, interface risks and acceptance closure.

  • Design and interface coordination across vendors
  • Execution quality checks and milestone verification
  • Acceptance support and closure governance

Operations and maintenance concept

Monitoring setup, preventive maintenance logic, KPI structure, issue workflows and long-term OPEX control.

  • Monitoring and KPI framework aligned to availability targets
  • Preventive maintenance concept and spare strategy
  • Issue handling workflow for faster recovery and warranty clarity

What we deliver

Structured documentation and evidence that makes projects easier to finance, easier to operate and easier to defend in case of disputes. Focus on interfaces, compliance, acceptance readiness and performance transparency.

  • Scope definition and interface responsibility matrix
  • Acceptance and commissioning evidence pack
  • Quality punch lists with closure tracking
  • Grid-compliance documentation set aligned to requirements
