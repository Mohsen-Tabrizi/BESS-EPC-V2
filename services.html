Services

Lifecycle Services for Utility-scale and C&I BESS

Clear scope, controlled interfaces and bankable evidence. We deliver projects that can be financed, connected, commissioned and operated with confidence.

1) Develop and de-risk

Before EPC starts, most losses are already baked in. We focus on grid reality, interfaces and a realistic scope.

Project development support

  • Site screening and grid feasibility (MV to EHV)
  • Permitting coordination, stakeholder alignment
  • RTB readiness: scope, schedule, risk list

Early technical concept

  • Sizing, topology and single-line concept
  • Interface matrix (BESS, PCS, transformer, MV/HV, control)
  • Risk-based design checks to avoid rework

Commercial-technical clarity

  • Scope boundaries and responsibilities (RACI)
  • Tender input and contract technical review
  • Acceptance criteria and evidence plan

2) Engineer, procure, build

Turnkey EPC plus structured engineering deliverables. The goal is predictable execution, not heroic firefighting.

Turnkey EPC(F)

  • Engineering, procurement, construction and integration
  • Quality control, site supervision, punch lists
  • Handover packs and as-built documentation

Engineering deliverables

  • Single Line Diagram (SLD) and protection concept
  • Cable lists, I/O lists, signal mapping
  • Interface control documents (ICD) and integration plan

Procurement and vendor control

  • Technical evaluation of offers (BESS, PCS, transformer)
  • Factory coordination, FAT planning and witness support
  • Incoming goods checks and documentation completeness

3) Connect, certify, commission

Grid compliance is not a checkbox. We make it measurable: requirements, tests, evidence.

Grid integration & compliance

  • Documentation aligned to VDE-AR-N 4110/4120/4130 and EN 50549
  • Protection and control verification
  • Test planning and evidence packs for operators and TSOs/DSOs

Commissioning support

  • Commissioning readiness review and test sequence
  • Defect tracking, closure, and acceptance readiness
  • Performance checks and transparent handover

Independent verification (project side)

  • Installation verification and interface checks
  • Evidence review against contract and standards
  • Bankable reporting for owners and lenders

4) Operate with control

A BESS is only valuable when it runs predictably. We set up monitoring and workflows that scale.

Monitoring & KPIs

  • Availability KPI structure and reporting logic
  • Alarm logic, event classification and escalation
  • Data integrity for trading and settlement

O&M concept

  • Preventive maintenance schedule and checklists
  • Spare parts logic and response planning
  • Warranty protection through documented operation

Owner’s engineering support

  • Independent representation for investors and owners
  • Claim-ready documentation for disputes
  • Portfolio-level scaling and standardisation
